Overview - MABS

        Maharaja Agrasen Business School (MABS) is a management institute located in New Delhi, India. It is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) and offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields of management. MABS is known for its academic excellence, faculty expertise, and industry-focused curriculum. The institute strives to provide students with a practical and real-world learning experience through its innovative teaching methods and industry partnerships. MABS has a dedicated placement cell that provides career guidance and placement assistance to its students. The institute has collaborations with leading companies and organizations in various sectors, and its graduates have been placed in top companies in India and abroad.

Placement

        Maharaja Agrasen Business School (MABS) has a dedicated placement cell that works towards providing placement opportunities to its students. The placement cell at MABS collaborates with various companies across different sectors, including FMCG, Banking & Finance, Consulting, IT, Healthcare, and Manufacturing, among others, to provide placement opportunities to its students. The placement process at MABS includes pre-placement talks, where companies present their organization and job profiles to the students, followed by the placement drive, where companies conduct interviews and select candidates based on their skills and suitability for the job.
